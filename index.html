Angela's Recipe for the Best Cinammon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 ½ tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup butter
